Questions

Everything you need
to know before starting

What is the minimum order quantity?

We are flexible, especially for first orders. Sample orders of 5-20 units are welcome to test quality before committing to larger volumes. Minimum order values vary by product category — contact us for specific details on your product of interest.

How do I know the quality will be consistent?

Every order is personally inspected by us in Casablanca before shipment. We photograph every piece and send you an approval gallery before anything leaves Morocco. Nothing ships without your explicit confirmation. We also work exclusively with verified suppliers who have demonstrated quality consistency.

How long does shipping take to Europe?

Sea freight from Casablanca to France, Belgium, Spain, Netherlands, and Portugal typically takes 7-14 days once the shipment is collected. Air freight is available for urgent orders and typically takes 3-5 days. We always build a production and quality check buffer into our quoted timelines.

Do you handle customs and export documentation?

Yes. We coordinate all export documentation on the Moroccan side through our established freight forwarding partners, including commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin, and EUR.1 certificates where applicable for preferential EU tariffs under the EU-Morocco Association Agreement.

Can I see samples before placing a full order?

Absolutely. We recommend starting with a sample order for any new product category. This allows you to verify quality, assess fit with your aesthetic, and build confidence in the supplier relationship before committing to larger volumes. Sample orders are fully credited against your first bulk order.

How do I pay and what currency?

We invoice in euros. Payment is by bank transfer — 50% deposit on order confirmation and 50% balance after quality inspection approval, before shipment. For established repeat clients, payment terms can be discussed. We use a professional European payment account for seamless international transfers.

Can you source products not in your catalogue?

Yes — custom sourcing is one of our core services. If you have a specific product brief, a reference image, or a concept you want to bring to life from Morocco, contact us with the details and we will assess feasibility, identify the right suppliers, and send you a sourcing proposal within 48 hours.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes. We specifically designed our service to be accessible for independent boutique businesses — not just large hotel chains or major retailers. If you are a small spa, an independent restaurant, or a new concept store looking for a reliable Moroccan sourcing partner, we are exactly the right fit.
